About RUBYFATH AID

Founded in 2024, RUBYFATH AID is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving lives in underserved communities across Ghana. Our mission is to create sustainable solutions to poverty through education, clean water, and community development.

Based in Offinso Ampabame, Ghana, we work directly with local communities to identify their most pressing needs and develop targeted interventions that make a lasting impact.

Our Board Members

Meet the dedicated individuals guiding RUBYFATH AID's mission and vision.

Mrs. Issahaku Rubabatu Kabore

CEO & Founder

Mrs. Rubabatu founded RUBYFATH AID in 2024 with a vision to transform communities in Ghana by helping them access their basic needs and beyond. She is a young health student with a deep passion for social work and community development, especially in supporting orphans and the underprivileged. Her leadership is marked by a strong commitment to transparency, accountability, and sustainable impact. Her drive for empowerment is inspired by her favorite Islamic quote from the Prophet (SAW): “I and the person who looks after an orphan and provides for him will be in Paradise.” ✌️.

Ayaaba Fatawu Sumaila

Executive Director

Fatawu brings over 1 year of experience in non-profit management, with a background in Computer Science and Engineering. He oversees daily operations and ensures that all programs align with the needs of the community, making the initiative a beacon of certainty and accountability. He finds joy in helping individuals discover their potential, regardless of their background. His ultimate aim is to put smiles on people’s faces and provide lasting solutions to challenges faced by the Ghanaian community.
